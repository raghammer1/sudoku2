How to Setup

1. To setup you sudoku or give the sudoku you want to be solved pick the numbers from the numbers panel then place them as per the question sudoku

2. If you place a number and don't want it there anymore or want to replace it then choose E and click there to remove it and then can replace it as well

3. Now finally to get it solved click on the solve now button and the solution will be presented

4. if you want to reset the whole grid to empty again press on the reset same button
